WELCOME

Our desire in Senoritos At Senoritas is that every person would have an encounter with God, that every hopeless situation would be turned around. With Gods' intervention, your mess would become your message, your test would become your testimony, and you would enjoy abundant life that Jesus came to bring. We are open to you. We believ that: even Life at 60, you can be young again because of a simple believe that there is no age with God

In the year 2015, God touched the hearts of a couple to begin a movement to reach out to the aging parents that would lead to the transformation of lives of often neglected seniors for the greater glory of God

Senoritos At Senoritas is creating a place for people to come and experience the new life found in Jesus Christ especially for the next generation. We have gatherings of youth and young adults who are inspired to live for Jesus! These gathering aim to empower today's generation - the high school and college students, the young professionals and the young married couples, with an identity found in Jesus Christ, live in the reality of His abounding grace, and experience first-hand God's glory, presence and power. Most of our gatherings are more about knitting of hearts, extraordinary corporate worship, fervent prayer, and an opportunity for leaders all over the city and the world to listen and hear from God through the Senior Pastors and anointed guest speakers

i-Connect is a daily morning gathering of believers to lift up and celebrate the Name above all names, Jesus! As Jesus is exalted, He people are drawn to Him and lives are touched and transformed. Jesus Festival is a celebration of who He is, what He's done, and who we are in Him. Jesus Festival is held in various locations in the country and in Asia. It is the earnest desire of our Senior Pastors to build closer, stronger connections with New Life pastors and leaders, and to inspire, encourage and equip them to press on towards Gods' great plans for the church, through immersion in the Word and divine encounters with the Holy Spirit even as look up to our spiritual fathers and leaders; Bishop Tony Marioghae and Bishop Carding Sio who unendless support in all spectrum brought us to where we are today.

Dr. Tony Marioghae and the Regional Director for TEAM Asia, Dr. Ricardo Sio launched the 'TEAM SENORITOS AT SENORITAS' as a branch of TEAM. A movement and Non-Government Organization (NGO) which actively champions the spiritual and moral reformation and the national transformation of vital institutions in the Republic of the Philippines.

TEAM attracts people from all walks of life, with a vision to transform the nation by advocating a productive paradigm shift. Since then, this revolutionary movement has attracted millions of people nationwide, equipped and transformed thousands of lives positively, and aggressively impacted the nation's political leaders, religious leaders, the business community, and the entertainment industry, in a way that only a few organizations ever did.
Thus leading to a huge spiritual harvest of souls, and massive revivals in different parts of the Philippines. Tony Marioghae believes that mental revolution is the key to a nation's transformation and sustainable development. He is a man of vision, integrity and excellence, who articulates his convictions passionately.
